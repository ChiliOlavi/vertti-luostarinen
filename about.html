About

I'm a media artist and AI researcher based in Helsinki. I work as a data scientist in the artist group Ekho Collective, building large-scale immersive installations. Our works include Nurjaton for Museum Eliel in Joensuu and Layers in the Peace Machine for the city of Oulu.

I'm a co-founder of a consultancy, CasvuGen, where I work on AI-related research and innovation projects for media industry clients. At CasvuGen, I have built neurodivergent chatbots and embedded tiny AI agents within game engines.

Interests

AI rhetoric, more-than-human intelligence, neurodiversity, panoramas, and asbestos.
